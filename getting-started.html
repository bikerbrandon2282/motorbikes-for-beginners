License Requirements

Your ultimate guide to starting your motorcycle journey. Whether you're a complete novice or just looking for some tips, we've got you covered.

First thing you will need to do is get your motorcycle license.
The requirements for this will vary depending on where you live, but generally, you will need to pass a written theory test and a practicalriding test.
This Website is based on the requirements for a motorcycle license in the United Kingdom.

  1. You will need to be at least 16 years old and hold a provisional driving license to start the process.
  2. Next, you will need to complete a Compulsory Basic Training (CBT) course, which is a one-day training program that covers the basics of motorcycle riding and safety. Once you have your CBT certificate you will be able to ride a motorcycle with L-plates on the road for 2 years.

Riding Restrictions

As a new rider there are certain restrictions you must adhere to.

  • All riders without a full motorcycle license must ride with 'L' Plates on front and rear at all times.
  • You can not carry passengers or ride on motorways until you have passed your full test.
  • If you are 16 years old you are restricted to a Motorcycle or moped with a maximum Engine Size of 50cc. If you are 17 years old you can ride a motorcycle with a maximum engine size of 125cc and a power output of 11kW.

Types of Licences

Once you pass your test you will have one of three full licenses depending on the size of the motorcycle you took your test with and your age.

  • If you took your test on a motorcycle with an engine size of 125cc or less (up to 11KW of power), you will receive an A1 license, which allows you to ride motorcycles up to 125cc. The minimum age for this license is 17 years old.
  • If you took your test on a motorcycle with a motorcycle of minimum engine size 245cc and between 11KW and 35kW of power, you will receive an A2 license, which allows you to ride motorcycles between 20kW and 35kW. The minimum age for this license is 19 years old.
  • If you took your test on a motorcycle with an engine size of at least 595cc and at least 50kW of power, you will receive an A license, which allows you to ride any motorcycle. The minimum age for this license is 24 years old, or 21 years old if you have held an A2 license for at least 2 years.
