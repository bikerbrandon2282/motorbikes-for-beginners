Types of Motorcycles
Sportbikes

Sportbikes are designed for speed and agility, with a focus on performance. They typically have a more aggressive riding position and are ideal for those who enjoy spirited riding on twisty roads or the track.

Dirtbikes

Dirtbikes are built for off-road adventures, with a focus on durability and performance in challenging terrain. They typically are ideal for those who enjoy riding on trails or in the dirt.

Cruiser Motorcycles

Cruisers are built for comfort and style, with a relaxed riding position and a focus on cruising at moderate speeds. They are ideal for long-distance travel and casual rides.

Standard Motorcycles

Standard motorcycles offer a balanced riding experience, combining comfort and performance. They are versatile and suitable for various riding conditions. Standard bikes are the most common and a great start for beginners.
